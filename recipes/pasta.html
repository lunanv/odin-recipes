Creamy Tuscan Chicken Pasta

Home

Description

A very creamy pasta recipe that you can easily prepare at home. This recipe takes approximately 35 minutes to prepare from start to finish.

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 skinless chicken breasts
  • salt & pepper
  • 8 cloves garlic
  • 1 cup sun-dried tomatoes
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 2 teasponns dried Italian seasoning
  • 2 cups grated Parmesan cheese
  • 4 cups fresh spinach leaves
  • (optional) 1/2 cup fresh basil leaves
