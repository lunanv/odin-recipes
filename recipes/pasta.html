Creamy Tuscan Chicken Pasta

Home

Description

A very creamy pasta recipe that you can easily prepare at home. This recipe takes approximately 35 minutes to prepare from start to finish.

Ingredients

  • Pasta of your choice
  • 4 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 skinless chicken breasts
  • salt & pepper
  • 8 cloves garlic
  • 1 cup sun-dried tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 2 teasponns dried Italian seasoning
  • 2 cups grated Parmesan cheese
  • 4 cups fresh spinach leaves
  • (optional) 1/2 cup fresh basil leaves, chopped

Steps

  1. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper on both sides.
  2. In a large skillet, cook the chicken breasts for around 5-7 minutes on each side over medium-high heat. Once cooked, remove the chicken from the skillet and set it aside on a plate. Cover with foil to keep warm.
  3. Prepare the pasta of your choice.
  4. Add olive oil to the same skillet you used to cook the chicken. Sauté over medium-high heat for about 1 minute until fragrant.
  5. Add the chopped sun-dried tomatoes and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.
  6. Pour in the chicken broth and bring it to a simmer. Let it cook for about 5 minutes, reducing the liquid by about half.
  7. Stir in the heavy cream and Italian seasoning. Bring the mixture to a gentle simmer.
  8. Gradually add the greated Parmesan cheese, stirring continuously until the sauce becomes smooth and thick.
  9. Season with salt and pepper to taste
  10. To finish the dish: combine the cooked pasta, fresh spinach, and basil leaves (if preferred) to the creamy sauce.
  11. Add slices of the cooked chicken breasts to the pasta.
  12. Serve and enjoy!
