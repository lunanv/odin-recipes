Chicken Karaage

Home

Description

An easy recipe for deep-fried chicken karaage. A deep fryer is not required. This recipe takes approximately 45 minutes to prepare from start to finish, but can vary depending on how long you decide to marinate the chicken.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb boneless skinless chicken thighs
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoons fresh ginger, grated
  • 1 quart peanut oil
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • salt & pepper
  • (optional) 1 scallion, sliced fine
  • (optional) 1 lemon, sliced into wedges for serving

Steps

  1. Trim excess fat from the chicken thighs and cut into rough 1-inch chunks.
  2. Combine chicken, soy sauce, garlic, and ginger in a bowl and mix until chicken is evenly coated in marinade.
  3. Cover and marinate in the fridge for at least 30 minutes.
  4. Heat peanut oil in an air fryer or large Dutch oven, or wok, set over high heat until the oil reaches 350°F.
  5. Meanwhile, combine cornstarch and flour in a bowl and mix. Remove chicken pieces from marinade and toss in flour mixture until well coated.
  6. While oil is hot, add chicken to oil. Stir occasionally with chopsticks or a wire mesh spider. Cook until chicken is cooked through and golden brown; around 5 to 7 minutes.
  7. Drain the fried chicken on a plate lined with paper towl and season immediately with salt.
  8. To finish the dish: garnish the chicken with scallion and lemon slices (optional).
  9. Serve and enjoy!
